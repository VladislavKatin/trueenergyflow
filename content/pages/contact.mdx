## What to include in your message

To help us recommend the best starting path, include:

1. the service you want (or ask for a recommendation)
2. your intention for the session in one or two sentences
3. preferred days/times and your time zone
4. any accessibility or communication preferences

If you are unsure where to begin, mention that and we will suggest a practical first step.
