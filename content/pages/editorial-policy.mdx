True Energy Flow publishes educational content for readers who want grounded guidance on energy healing, remote healing, intuitive readings, craniosacral therapy, and spiritual coaching. We do not publish for shock value or certainty theater. We publish to help readers make clearer, calmer, more responsible decisions.

## What we publish

Our content focuses on:

- session expectations and preparation
- reflective and spiritual guidance
- practical aftercare and integration
- coaching and regulation-oriented support
- safe framing for health-adjacent spiritual topics

We prioritize topics where readers commonly need plain language, realistic expectations, and better questions.

## How we write

Each article is shaped around a few editorial rules:

- use plain English before niche jargon
- define scope clearly
- avoid inflated claims
- connect insight to real behavior and decision-making
- keep medical, legal, and mental health boundaries explicit

If a topic touches health-related language, we narrow the scope further and use non-medical framing.

## How review works

Articles may include a visible editorial author line and a safety review note. Safety review is focused on:

- responsible non-medical language
- claim restraint
- practical clarity
- alignment between the article title and the actual body content
- internal linking to the most relevant service or educational follow-up pages

We also use references where they improve trust, context, or safety.

## What we avoid

We do not knowingly publish content that:

- guarantees healing outcomes
- claims to diagnose illness
- pressures readers through fear
- discourages licensed medical or mental health support
- uses spiritual language to override practical judgment

If a claim would require clinical proof or licensure we do not have, it does not belong in our editorial voice.

## How readers should use this site

Use this site for education, orientation, reflection, and session preparation. Do not use it as a substitute for diagnosis, treatment, emergency care, or professional mental health support.

If you want to understand our boundaries in direct language, also review the [Disclaimer](/disclaimer) and [Safety Policy](/safety-policy).

## Corrections and updates

We update content when we find clearer wording, better supporting sources, or a better way to match reader intent without overstating what the content can responsibly do.
