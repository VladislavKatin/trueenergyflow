Safety is not a footer detail on this site. It is part of how every service page, article, and client-facing explanation is written.

## Core safety principle

True Energy Flow provides spiritual and educational support. It does not provide medical diagnosis, medical treatment, emergency care, or psychotherapy.

That means our content should help people become more observant, more grounded, and more responsible, not more dependent on spiritual certainty.

## How we handle health-adjacent topics

Some readers search for terms such as medical intuitive, medical medium, or symptom-related spiritual guidance. On this site, those topics are framed as:

- reflective support
- intuitive guidance
- pattern awareness
- better question formation for licensed care
- emotional grounding during uncertainty

They are not framed as diagnosis.

## What responsible guidance looks like

Responsible guidance should:

- encourage discernment
- keep claims modest
- point readers back to licensed care when needed
- avoid pressure and fear
- support practical follow-through

Good spiritual support does not ask people to abandon reality. It helps them meet reality more clearly.

## Red lines we do not cross

We do not publish or endorse content that:

- claims to cure disease
- replaces doctors or therapists
- tells readers to stop treatment or medication
- uses urgency to create dependence
- treats intuition as a substitute for evaluation of urgent symptoms

## When licensed care should come first

Licensed care should remain central when symptoms are:

- severe
- worsening
- persistent
- medically concerning
- affecting safety or daily function
- tied to mental health risk or crisis

In those cases, spiritual content should only play a supporting role, if any.

## Session safety

For services, we aim to keep expectations realistic. A useful session should help a client identify one or two meaningful next steps, not create the illusion that every issue can be resolved through one spiritual encounter.
