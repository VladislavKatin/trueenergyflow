## Overview

Intuitive Readings are for people who want clearer perspective on decisions, patterns, and personal direction. This service is focused on insight with boundaries: useful guidance, grounded language, and practical next steps.

A strong reading should increase your agency, not reduce it. The goal is to help you see your options more clearly and move forward with intention.

If you are comparing formats, read [intuitive reading vs psychic reading](/blog/intuitive-reading-vs-psychic-reading).

## What this service helps with

Common focus areas include:

- recurring relationship patterns
- career crossroads and timing decisions
- personal purpose and values alignment
- boundaries, burnout, and emotional overload

Sessions are collaborative. You bring your real context, and we work with specific questions rather than vague predictions.

## Reading format

1. **Question framing**  
   We define one or two high-quality questions for the session.

2. **Intuitive exploration**  
   We work through impressions, patterns, and practical meaning.

3. **Action translation**  
   We convert insight into a short decision framework you can use immediately.

## What to bring to your session

- one current decision you feel stuck on
- short timeline context (what changed and when)
- your own observations, not just assumptions
- openness to practical follow-through

Intuitive Readings are spiritual and educational. They do not diagnose conditions, prescribe treatment, or replace licensed clinical care.

## FAQ

### Is this predictive?

The focus is clarity and alignment, not certainty claims. We prioritize actionable insight over prediction language.

### Can this replace therapy or medical care?

No. This service should complement, not replace, licensed support.

### How often should I book?

Many clients start with one session and then decide on follow-up based on integration and real-life outcomes.
