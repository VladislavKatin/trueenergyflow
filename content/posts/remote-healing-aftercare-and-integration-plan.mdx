## The first 48 hours

Your first two days should be low-noise and high-awareness. Do less, observe more.

## Days 3-5: convert insight into behavior

By day three, translate insight into one visible action and one boundary shift.

## Day 7: review and adjust

On day seven, assess honestly: what changed, what did not, and what support you need next.

## What to do this week

1. Write one sentence intention.
2. Choose one action you can complete today.
3. Review results in seven days.
