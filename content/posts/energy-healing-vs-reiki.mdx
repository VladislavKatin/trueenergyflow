## Where the overlap ends

Reiki is a specific method; energy healing is the wider category. The confusion starts when people compare labels instead of experience design.

## How to choose by session style

If you prefer quiet consistency, Reiki may feel better. If you want dialogue and tailored integration, broader energy work may fit.
