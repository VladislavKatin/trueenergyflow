## Preparation should reduce friction

You do not need elaborate rituals. You need quiet, timing, and one sentence of intention.

## During-session mindset

During the session, keep your role simple: observe breath, body, and emotional pace without forcing meaning.

## The six-hour aftercare window

The first evening matters. Lower stimulation, hydrate, and capture one actionable insight before sleep.

## What to do this week

1. Write one sentence intention.
2. Choose one action you can complete today.
3. Review results in seven days.
