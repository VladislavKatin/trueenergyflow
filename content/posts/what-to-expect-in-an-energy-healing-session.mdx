## What the first 10 minutes tell you

The opening conversation is where a good practitioner earns trust. You should hear plain language, clear boundaries, and a focus on your real life, not vague promises.

## What happens in the middle of the session

In the middle phase, most clients notice small but meaningful shifts: breathing slows, thought loops soften, and emotional intensity drops enough to think clearly again.

## What to do right after

After the session, the key is integration. One practical action within 24 hours is more useful than ten abstract insights.

## What to do this week

1. Write one sentence intention.
2. Choose one action you can complete today.
3. Review results in seven days.
