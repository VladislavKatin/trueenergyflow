## Safe framing in one sentence

Intuitive medical guidance can support reflection and better questions, but it cannot diagnose, treat, or replace clinical care.

## Where intuitive guidance helps

It can be useful when people feel overwhelmed and need language to communicate with licensed professionals.

## Where the line must stay firm

If a practitioner presents certainty about diagnosis or treatment outcomes, step back immediately.
