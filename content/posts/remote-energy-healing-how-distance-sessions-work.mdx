## Why distance is not the deciding factor

Remote sessions work when attention is focused and structure is clear. Physical distance matters less than session quality and follow-through.

## How remote sessions stay effective

The best format is straightforward: short check-in, guided regulation, and one clear integration plan.

## How to evaluate results honestly

Evaluate over a week, not an hour. Look at sleep trend, decision quality, and stress recovery speed.
