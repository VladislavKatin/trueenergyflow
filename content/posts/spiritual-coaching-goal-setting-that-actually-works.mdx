## Why most goals fail in week two

Goals usually fail from poor design, not poor character. Vague goals collapse under normal stress.

## How to design goals that survive real life

Set one specific target, one timeline, and two measurable indicators.

## Weekly execution over motivation

Execution is a weekly rhythm, not a mood.

## What to do this week

1. Write one sentence intention.
2. Choose one action you can complete today.
3. Review results in seven days.
